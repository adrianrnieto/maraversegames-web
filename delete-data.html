Data Deletion Request

The Wildlife Collection · Last updated: 2 July 2026

The Wildlife Collection is a single-player game that does not use accounts. This page explains how to delete the two kinds of data associated with the app.

1. Your game data (on your device)

Your entire game progress — collection, coins, quests, achievements, streaks, and settings — is stored only on your device. To delete all of it, simply uninstall the app, or clear its storage in Settings › Apps › The Wildlife Collection › Storage › Clear data. We never receive a copy, so there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

2. Data collected by third-party services

The app sends a limited amount of pseudonymous data to third parties to run and improve the game: anonymous usage analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase), rewarded-ads data including the advertising identifier (Google AdMob), and crash diagnostics (Sentry). This data is not tied to your name or any account.

To request deletion of this data, email support@maraversegames.com from any address, with the subject "Data deletion request". We will action it within 30 days. It helps if you include your device model and the approximate date you last played so we can locate the relevant records.

You can also limit this data yourself at any time by resetting or deleting your device's advertising identifier in Settings › Privacy › Ads, and by clearing the app's data as described above.

The Wildlife Collection ("the app"), published by Maraverse Games, is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

All of your game progress — collected cards, coins, settings and statistics — is stored locally on your device. The app is playable fully offline.

Data we collect

  • Google Play Games (if you sign in): used for cloud save and leaderboards, subject to Google's privacy policy.

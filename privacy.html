Privacy Policy

Wildlife Collection · Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wildlife Collection ("the app", "we", "us"), published by Maraverse Games, handles your information. The app is a single-player wildlife card-collecting game. Your game progress stays on your device; the only information that leaves it is the limited data described below, used to keep the app running, improve it, and show rewarded ads.

1. Data stored on your device

All of your game progress — your collection, coins, quests, achievements, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself (see "Exporting your data" below). We do not run a game server, and we cannot see, access, or recover your game progress.

2. Analytics

To understand how the app is used and to improve it, we use Google Analytics for Firebase. It records anonymous usage events (for example, opening a pack, completing a collection, or changing a setting) together with a pseudonymous, app-generated instance identifier and general technical information such as your device model, operating-system version, app version, language, and approximate country or region. This data does not include your name, email, or the contents of your collection, and it is not used to personally identify you. Google processes this data as described in Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase's privacy information.

3. Advertising

The app offers optional rewarded video ads (for example, to earn coins or an extra pack) served through Google AdMob. To show and measure these ads, Google may use your device's advertising identifier and related information. You are never required to watch an ad to play.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we show a Google-provided consent form the first time it is needed, so you can choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device's system settings. See how Google uses data for AdMob and how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

4. Crash and error diagnostics

To find and fix bugs, the app uses Sentry (sentry.io/privacy), a third-party error-reporting service. When the app crashes or hits an unexpected error, a diagnostic report is sent to Sentry. These reports contain technical information such as the error message and stack trace, the app version, your device model, and operating-system version. They do not include your name or any data you would consider personally identifying, and they do not include the contents of your game collection.

5. Notifications

With your permission, the app schedules local notifications on your device (for example, to remind you when a free pack is ready). These are generated and stored entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server. You can disable them at any time in the app's Settings or in your device's system settings.

6. Exporting your data

The app lets you export your game progress to a file so you can back it up or move it to another device. When you do this, the exported file is handled by your device's own sharing tools and goes wherever you choose to send it. We never receive a copy.

7. Data we do not collect

  • We do not require an account or ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, or files.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

8. Children's privacy

The app is directed to a general audience and is not intended for children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are below the age of digital consent where you live, please use the app together with a parent or guardian.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Deleting your data

Your game progress lives only on your device — uninstalling the app or clearing its storage removes it entirely. To request deletion of the limited data collected by third-party services (analytics, ads, crash diagnostics), see our Data Deletion page.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@maraversegames.com.

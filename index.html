About the studio

Small studio, big worlds

Maraverse Games is an independent studio that builds beautiful, respectful games about the things worth knowing — real wildlife, the night sky, the deep past, and worlds we dream up ourselves.

We care about craft: genuine data, hand-tuned art, and a daily loop that feels good to come back to. No dark patterns, no clutter — just the quiet joy of filling a collection.

🐾 The name carries a little piece of home: Mara, our dog — and the mark you'll find on everything we make.
